# Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and welcoming environment for all contributors.

- Be respectful and considerate
- Assume good intentions
- Provide constructive feedback
- Avoid harassment and discrimination

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainers.

This project follows a simplified version of the Contributor Covenant. Severe or repeated violations may result in temporary or permanent bans from project spaces.
